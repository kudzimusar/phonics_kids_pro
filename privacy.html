Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

Phonics Kids Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information in our application.

1. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is designed for children aged 3-8. We strictly comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not collect personally identifiable information from children without parental consent.

2. Voice Data & AI Processing

Phonics Kids Pro uses audio recording to provide real-time pronunciation feedback ("Phonics Cast"). Audio data is processed using Google Vertex AI.

  • Audio data is used solely for grading pronunciation.
  • We do not store audio recordings on our servers after processing.
  • Audio data is not used for advertising or profiling.

3. Information Collection

We collect minimal data to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Parent's email and encrypted password (managed via Firebase Auth).
  • Learning Progress: Lesson completion, scores, and "Benjamin Notebook" entries to help children track their growth.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (Firestore Rules and Firebase Security) to protect your data. We never share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights

Parents can request to review or delete their child's data at any time by contacting our support team.
